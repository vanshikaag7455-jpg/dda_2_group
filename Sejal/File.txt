# DATA ANALYTICS
Data Analytics is the process of examining raw data to uncover meaningful insights, patterns, and trends that support decision-making.
It involves techniques such as data cleaning, transformation, visualization, and statistical analysis to convert data into actionable information.

## Benefits of Data Analytics:

Improved Decision-Making: Helps organizations make data-driven and evidence-based decisions.

Enhanced Efficiency: Identifies bottlenecks and optimizes operations and processes.

Cost Reduction: Detects inefficiencies and reduces unnecessary expenses.

Customer Insights: Analyzes customer behavior and preferences to improve satisfaction and retention.

Risk Management: Predicts potential risks and frauds using historical data patterns.

Innovation: Reveals new business opportunities and market trends.
